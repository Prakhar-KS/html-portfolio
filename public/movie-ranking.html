The Best Movies According to Prakhar

My top 3 movies of all time

Interstellar

I loved how deep this movies goes regarding space and science. The concept behind this movie is also quite futuristic.

Oppenheimer

One more work of Christopher Nolan makes into my list of top 3 movies. It deeply explains the amount of power that a atom bomb has. Cilian Murphy as Oppenheimer has done a brilliant role.

3 Idiots

Its a hindi movie that explains what should be the actual process of learning for the students so that they can excel in their life. I loved how this movie tells the viewers to run after knowledge rather than marks.
